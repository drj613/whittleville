Our Services

What we do and how we do it

Woodworking

We build furniture, trim, cabinetry, and just about anything else you can make from wood. We know how to work with different species and grains, and we'll help you pick the right material for whatever you've got in mind.

Services include custom furniture design and build, built-in cabinetry, wood trim and molding, specialty woodworking projects, and wood selection consulting.

Deck Repairs & Refinishing

We design and build decks that actually hold up. Good materials, solid framing, and the kind of finish work that can take years of weather without falling apart. We can also fix up or refinish a deck that's seen better days.

Services include custom deck design and construction, deck repair and refinishing, railing installation, composite and wood decking options, and permit assistance.

Furniture Restoration

Got an old dresser from your grandparents? A table you found at an estate sale? We can fix it up. We handle structural repairs, refinishing, and hardware replacement, and we keep the character of the original piece intact.

Services include structural repairs and stabilization, wood refinishing and staining, hardware replacement, veneer repair and replacement, and period-appropriate restoration techniques.

General Handiwork

Something loose, stuck, or just not right? We handle the small stuff too. Shelving, door repairs, mounting, weather stripping, you name it. No job's too minor if it's been bugging you.

Painting & Color Consultation

We do interior and exterior painting, staining, and can help you pick colors that actually work with your space. Joey's got an eye for what belongs in a room, and he's not afraid to tell you if that accent wall isn't it.

Landscaping

From garden beds to retaining walls, we can shape up your yard and keep it looking intentional. We work with what's already there when we can and build out from scratch when we need to.

Porches, Pergolas & Gazebos

Outdoor structures that look good and hold up. We design and build porches, pergolas, and gazebos that fit the house they're attached to, not just dropped in from a catalog.

Home Inspection (coming soon)

Joey's working toward his home inspection certification. This one's not available yet, but it's on the way.
