About Joey Atwell

Founder & Designer

Joey learned woodworking in his grandfather's shop. He picked up the basics there, but more importantly, he learned to slow down and do things right. That stuck with him. Now he runs Whittleville, where he builds custom furniture, decks, and brings beat-up old pieces back to life.

Got a project in mind? Joey will work with you to figure out what you need and how to get there. He takes his time with every cut and every joint, because the details are what make the difference.
